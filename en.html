FAQ
  1. Will there be transportation to some hotels?

    During the night there will be a transportation service that will go to the Aquetzalli Hotel and the Hacienda de Cocoyoc. You can also find taxis outside the venue and sometimes there are Ubers in the area.

  2. Is there any type of discount with some hotels?

    Hotel Hacienda Cocoyoc has a discount but please contact us so we can help you with your reservation.

  3. Will there be parking?

    The Hacienda has a valet parking service.

  4. At what time does the wedding start?

    The ceremony will be held inside the Hacienda at 1:30pm, so in case you want to participate please arrive on time. The reception will begin at 2:30pm, this is also inside the Hacienda.

  5. Can children attend?

    Unfortunately, children will not be able to accompany us. However, some hotels have a babysitter service in case you would like to take their children and leave them at your hotel.

  6. Will there be a vegetarian menu?

    There will be some vegetarian options, however we will not have a vegan menu. Also, if you are allergic to any type of food let us know as soon as possible.

  7. What is the deadline for RSVP?

    It would be very helpful if it was before September 1st, 2021. In case you have any specific situation, please contact us directly.

  8. What protective measures will we take against COVID?

    So that we can all be safe, we ask if you can get an antigen test one or two days before the wedding. Then, someone will contact you to keep a record of all the guests.
