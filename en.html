FAQ
  1. Will there be transportation to some hotels?

    At the moment there is no transportation service to any of the hotels, but the Hacienda is near a taxi site, and there are some ubers around the area.

  2. Is there any type of discount with some hotels?

    Hotel Hacienda Cocoyoc is the only one with a discount when mentioning our names but you need to reserve a minimum of two nights.

  3. Will there be parking?

    The Hacienda has a valet parking service.

  4. At what time does the wedding start?

    The ceremony will be held inside the Hacienda at 1pm, so we ask that if you want to participate please arrive before that time. Later, the reception will begin at 3pm, this is also inside the Hacienda.

  5. Can children attend?

    Unfortunately, children will not be able to accompany us since the venue has areas that are dangerous, However, some hotels have a babysitter service in case they would like to take their children and leave them at their hotel.

  6. Will there be a vegetarian menu?

    There will be some vegetarian options, however we will not have a vegan menu. Also, if you are allergic to any type of food let us know as soon as possible.

  7. What is the deadline for RSVP?

    It would be very helpful if it was before September 1st, 2021. In case you have any specific situation, please contact us directly.
